WHAT IS THIS EVENT

Upper Thamae charity drive is a community event held in Ha Thamae, Maseru, to support vulnerable famalies and chrildren.The event brings together local business, volunteers, students, and community leaders so to donate food, clothes , school suplies, and essentail households items to familes in need.

The event also includes entertainment, motivational talks, wellness activities, and fundraising programs aimed at improving the lives of people within the community. Our goal is to promote unity, kindnes,and social responsibilities while creating a positive impact in maseru.

WHAT MOTIVATED US TO FORM THIS EVENT

Lesotho Red Cross Society is one of the organizatins that showed us that we can also change and bring light in our society by giving the need what we have.
